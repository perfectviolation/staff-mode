Features /staff command which

- Sets the players gamemode to creative
- Clears their inventory/armor and returns it once they exit staff mode.
- Makes the user vanish


IMPORTANT:
DUE TO A MISTAKE ON MY PART, THE PLUGIN IS LISTED UNDER THE NAME "CUSTOM",
YOU'LL PROBABLY SEE IT IN /PLUGINS

MAKE SURE TO GIVE STAFF RANKS THAT YOU WANT TO HAVE THE ABILITY TO SEE OTHER STAFF MEMBERS WHILE VANISHED THE staffmod.see PERMISSION


Permissions:

staffmode.use        --> Gives access to the /staff command
staffmod.see         --> Gives ability to see vanished players in /staff mode, can be used if for example you don't want helpers to see other vanished staff members.
